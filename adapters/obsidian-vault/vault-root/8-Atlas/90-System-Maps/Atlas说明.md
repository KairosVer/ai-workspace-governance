# Atlas 说明

`8-Atlas` is the map and navigation layer of the vault.

It does not store primary knowledge. It organizes routes across Inbox, Wiki, Projects, Outputs, Archive, and rules.

## Principle

Folders answer "where does this live?" Atlas answers "how do I find my way through it?"
