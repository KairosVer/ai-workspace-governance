# Positioning

AI Workspace Governance is not a note-taking system, a project management app, or an AI prompt collection.

It is a lightweight operating protocol for folders that AI agents are allowed to touch.

## One Sentence

A lightweight operating system for folders that humans and AI agents manage together.

## Short Pitch

When AI can change files, your workspace needs a constitution. This project gives you a practical one: where files go, what agents must read, what requires human confirmation, and how every AI operation is logged.

## What Makes It Different

- Tool-agnostic first, adapters second.
- Rules are executable by agents, not just inspirational for humans.
- Safety and audit logs are part of the default workflow.
- It works for knowledge bases, research folders, writing projects, and documentation repositories.
- It treats AI as a collaborator with boundaries, not as a magic cleanup button.

## Naming Ideas for Adopters

People can describe their setup as:

- an AI-governed workspace

- an agent-ready folder system
- a workspace constitution for AI agents
- a review-first AI file workflow
